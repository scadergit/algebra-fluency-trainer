# FEATURES.md

# Algebra Fluency Trainer Feature Roadmap

This document defines the desired functionality of the Algebra Fluency Trainer.

The goal is to build the highest quality Algebra practice application available for students, teachers, and parents.

This is a living document.

---

# Project Goals

The application should:

- Build mathematical fluency.
- Encourage daily practice.
- Require minimal teacher supervision.
- Adapt to student ability.
- Be enjoyable without being distracting.
- Support students from elementary arithmetic through Algebra I.

---

# Core Principles

The application should always be:

- Fast
- Keyboard friendly
- Responsive
- Accessible
- Predictable
- Easy to use

The interface should never get in the student's way.

---

# Practice Modes

## Standard Practice

Unlimited practice problems.

Immediate feedback.

Statistics tracked.

---

## Timed Practice

Student selects:

- 30 seconds
- 1 minute
- 2 minutes
- 5 minutes
- 10 minutes

Statistics include:

- problems completed
- accuracy
- average response time

---

## Target Score

Practice until:

- 20 correct
- 50 correct
- 100 correct

---

## Target Accuracy

Continue until:

- 90%
- 95%
- 100%

---

## Speed Drill

Focus entirely on automaticity.

Very fast transitions.

Minimal animations.

---

## Mixed Review

Randomly select problems from multiple enabled skills.

Weighted by mastery.

---

## Adaptive Practice

Increase difficulty automatically.

Reduce difficulty after repeated mistakes.

Favor weak skills.
